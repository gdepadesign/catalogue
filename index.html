“Back to Prison” Antigone’s 13th report on detention conditions in Italy.

A report divided into four major areas: policies and numbers; emergencies; who lives inside; and who works inside. Each area has within it insights into the situation in Italian prisons that focus on issues such as: numbers, costs, staffing, suicides, religious freedom and radicalisation, women, foreigners, etc.
